Contribute your notes

At Athenas, we foster a global community of learning and collaboration. We encourage you to share your valuable notes and knowledge with fellow students around the world. By contributing your notes, you have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on someone's educational journey, regardless of their location. Your notes could be the missing piece to help a student excel in their studies.

How do I contribute my notes?

Simply reach out to us via email at support@athenas.com. Our dedicated team will guide you through the process and ensure that your valuable notes are shared with students worldwide.

What details should I include?

When submitting your notes, please make sure to include essential details such as the topic or subject matter, a brief description of the content, the date of creation, and most importantly, your name as the author. These details will help us organize and categorize the notes effectively, ensuring that they reach the right students seeking valuable resources.

Will I get the credit?

Absolutely! We value your contributions, and you will receive full credit for the notes you provide. Your name will be acknowledged as the author of the notes, ensuring that your efforts and expertise are recognized within our community. We appreciate your dedication in sharing your knowledge to benefit students worldwide.

What if I wanna update/delete my notes?

Simply send us a message detailing the changes or deletion request, and our team will assist you promptly. We'll make the necessary updates or remove the notes as per your instructions and keep you informed about the status of your request. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we are here to accommodate any necessary modifications to ensure the accuracy and quality of the shared resources.
